About Us

Building the future with strength, precision, and innovation since our founding in Japan.

Excellence in Steel Manufacturing

SCG Metals Co., Inc. is a premier steel materials and fabrication company headquartered in the heart of Tokyo, Japan. With decades of expertise, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for construction, infrastructure, and industrial projects worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art facilities in Otemachi combine traditional Japanese craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver steel products that meet the highest international standards — JIS, ASTM, and ISO certified.

From standard structural steel to complex custom fabrications, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to your exact specifications, dimensions, and design requirements.

Our Services

End-to-end steel solutions from design and fabrication to delivery and installation support.

Custom Fabrication

Precision fabrication of steel components to your exact specifications. We handle any size and design complexity with state-of-the-art CNC machinery.

  • CNC cutting and bending
  • Welding (MIG, TIG, Arc)
  • Drilling and punching
  • Surface treatment (galvanizing, painting)
  • Assembly and kitting

Structural Design

Engineering support for structural steel projects. Our team provides detailed drawings, load calculations, and design optimization.

  • Structural analysis
  • 3D modeling and BIM
  • Connection design
  • Code compliance review
  • Shop drawings

Logistics & Delivery

Global shipping and logistics management. We ensure your materials arrive on time, every time, with full tracking and documentation.

  • International freight
  • Customs clearance
  • Just-in-time delivery
  • Warehousing solutions
  • Project site delivery

Quality Testing

Comprehensive material testing and certification. Every batch is tested to ensure compliance with international standards.

  • Chemical composition analysis
  • Mechanical property testing
  • Ultrasonic testing (UT)
  • Radiographic testing (RT)
  • Mill test certificates

Prototyping

Rapid prototyping services for custom steel components. Validate your designs before full production with our quick-turn sample service.

  • 3D printing (metal)
  • Sample batch production
  • Design iteration support
  • Performance testing
  • Cost optimization

Installation Support

Technical support for on-site installation. Our engineers provide guidance to ensure proper assembly and safety compliance.

  • On-site technical consultation
  • Installation manuals
  • Training programs
  • Quality inspection
  • After-sales support
